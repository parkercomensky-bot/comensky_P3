Magical Melodies

This activity explores music creation using the free chrome music labs website. Users will work together to create a song using ten bars of music, with the song maker option on the website. The song will be two bars, and contain notes and beats combined to make their own custom song. The goal of this game is to work collaboratively, and use teamwork to create something tangible that all players contributed to.

Tools

  • One phone or computer
  • Chrome Music Labs website
  • Timer

Number of Participants

3-5

Time for Exercise

10 Minutes

Instructions

  1. Take out one players phone and pull up chrome music labs
  2. Tap on song maker and go to settings
  3. Set the number of bars to four and press the check mark
  4. Players can decide on what they want the musical instrument to sound like. The options are:
    • Marimba
    • piano
    • Strings
    • Woodwind
    • Synth
  5. Players can choose the beat from the following:
    • Electronic
    • Blocks
    • Kit
    • Conga
  6. Once players decide on the settings, the settings must stay the same for the duration of the activity
  7. Players are allowed to place as many notes as they want, as long as it stays in its own column
  8. Players are suggested to only place one beat in each column (a triangle or a circle)
  9. Set a five minute timer and press start
  10. Do not play the song until the timer is completed
  12. The first player will place a note and a beat
  13. Pass the phone counter clockwise, and have the second player place a note and a beat
  14. Repeat step 12 until all of the bars are filled with notes and beats
  15. Once time is up, play the song out loud
  16. Players have now created their own song collaboratively and can listen to it back as many times as desired
  17. Click the share button, and download as a WAV file, to have your song saved to replay as much as you wish.
